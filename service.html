Towson University Courses

These are the courses I took that are essential in the field of Software Engineering and Data Science.

Introduction To Computer Science I and II

Taught the basics of Computer Science and Object Oriented Design. I worked on multiple projects in these classes that helped prepare me for the higher level Com Sci Classes.

Calculus I and II

Learned the basics of calculus and how to apply it in real life scenarios, this knowledge can be used when performing computational work on computer.

Principles of Computer Organization

Learned more about the computer on the hardware level, and how it operates from low level code to high level code. Also did programming in assembly during lab.

Data Structures and Algorithmns

Learned about various data structures (heaps, trees, stacks, queues, etc.) and different algorithmns that can be used for programming.

Statistics

I learned that statistics is a powerful tool for analyzing and interpreting data. It involves various methods to summarize and draw conclusions from data sets. Understanding statistics helps in making informed decisions based on evidence rather than assumptions.

Discrete Mathematics

I learned different proofs and how they correlate in both real life and on computers. Went over different theories and how math equations work logically.
